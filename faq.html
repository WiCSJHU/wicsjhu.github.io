FAQ

I'm new to WiCS.
Check out our welcome guide here.
What does it mean to be a WiCS member/active member?

If you're a member (i.e., you've attended 3 WiCS events), you're officially part of our organization and we'll put your name (and bio, if you'd like) on the website! Besides that:

Members vs. Nonmembers

Both members and nonmembers can attend general events (Coding Circle, or anything else on our monthly events calendar), but members get additional access to the ACM/WiCS lounge and the WiCS resources database.

Active Members vs. Members

Active members (members who attend at least 2 events per semester) receive invites to exclusive company events and can participate in our mentorship program.

I'm a member but I haven't attended >2 events this semester.

Then your name is probably under the "Past Members" section on our About page. Once you attend 2 events, we'll bump you up to the "Active Members" section and add you to the invite list for exclusive events.

If you don't attend >2 events, you'll remain a nonactive member for this semester,but you'll still be able to become an active member next semester (by attending >2 events then).
