The OlympiCS

JHU's annual CS games for undergrads! Sign up // Learn more

The OlympiCS is an annual, day-long tradition at Johns Hopkins University. Teams of CS undergrads compete in fun games and earn points towards prizes. Past OlympiCS events include: Pictionary, Wikipedia races, Family Feud, and (of course) algorithm dancing.

The 2nd annual OlympiCS will take place during this semester's reading period: Saturday, May 5, 2018 @1-6pm in Malone Hall. Prizes TBA.

Sign up for the 2nd annual OlympiCS here!

Interested in volunteering? Join our planning committee here.


The OlympiCS is hosted by ACM (Association of Computing Machinery) and WiCS (Women in Computer Science, aka the ACM-Women chapter), JHU's undergraduate CS groups.

Questions? Contact us at jhucsgames@gmail.com.
