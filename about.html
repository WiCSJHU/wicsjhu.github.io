About

Women in CS (WiCS) is Johns Hopkins University's official ACM-W chapter. We are run by and for undergraduates--particularly for women/non-binary individuals, as well as other underrepresented groups in computing.

WiCS events include weekly "Coding Circles," company talks, student panels/presentations, study sessions/study breaks, and a mentorship program.
