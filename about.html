About WiCS


Women in Computer Science (WiCS) is the ACM-W Chapter at Johns Hopkins University. The organization is run by and consists of undergraduates who are majoring, minoring, or simply interested in computer science.

We meet for Coding Circle every Friday at 4:30pm in Malone 228.
